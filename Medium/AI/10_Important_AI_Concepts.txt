1. Tokens
How AI reads text
tokenization = divide sentence into tokens


2. Embeddings
How AI understands meaning
text converted into numbers
numbers = embeddings


3. Attention
How AI understands context
meaning of word depends on context


4. Transformers
The engine behind modern AI
transformers changed AI forever
when you chat w/ AI = predict next word v/ fast


5. LLMs
Large Language Model
transformer trained on massive amounts of text = predict next token

6. Hallucination
When AI sounds right but is wrong


7. Temperature
Controls creativity vs. Accuracy
AI gradually predicts the next most likely token
temperature controls how random that selection is


8. Context Window
AI memory
AI only sees what is inside context window


9. RAG
Retrieval Augmented Generation
rather than relying solely on what AI was trained on
AI looks for pertinent documents before providing a response
so
more accurate, more up-to-date = more reliable


10.AI Agents
AI that can take actions
AI normal	answers
AI agent	plans, uses tools, executes, checks, repeats, finish task
